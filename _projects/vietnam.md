## About this project

This project examines media systems and the circulation of misinformation in
Vietnam, combining fieldwork with computational analysis of online platforms.
It investigates how information moves through the Vietnamese media
environment, how audiences encounter and respond to it, and what this implies
for media effects and platform governance in the region.

The project is carried out in collaboration with colleagues across the
University of Sydney and partners in Vietnam.
