## About this project

This project investigates "underspheres" — emerging spaces and practices of
generative AI misuse — and their implications for democratic communication,
trust, and the integrity of the public sphere. It explores how synthetic
content is produced and circulated, the challenges it poses for audiences and
institutions, and the governance responses it calls for.
