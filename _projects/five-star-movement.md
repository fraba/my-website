## About this project

This long-running strand of research examines Italy's Five Star Movement
(M5S) as a paradigmatic case of the "digital party". It traces how online
participation, intra-party democracy, candidate selection, and leadership
influence are shaped by the affordances of the multiple platforms the
movement has relied on — from Beppe Grillo's Blog and the M5S forum and
Meetup to Facebook and the party's online primaries.

Across these studies the project asks what digital platforms do to the
internal life of political organisations, and what the M5S experience implies
for participatory democracy and the role of parties in democratic systems
more broadly.
