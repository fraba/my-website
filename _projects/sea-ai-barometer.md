## About this project

This collaborative project co-designs a Southeast Asian AI Barometer: a
framework and instrument for assessing whether, and how, artificial
intelligence serves public infrastructure across the region. Working with
partners in Southeast Asia, it aims to surface regional priorities and
concerns and to build a shared evidence base on AI's public value.
